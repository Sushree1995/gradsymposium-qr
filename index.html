Oral Presentation Guidelines

  • Presentation Length: 12 minutes for presentation followed by 3 minutes for Q&A.
  • Recommended Format:
    • Title Slide
    • Introduction
    • Materials and Methods
    • Results and Discussion
    • Conclusions
    • Literature Cited
    • Acknowledgments
  • Technical Guidelines:
    • Titles: 40 points
    • Main Points: 32 points
    • Secondary Points: 28 points
  • Tips:
    • Know your audience.
    • Practice and stick to the time limit.
    • Use clear visuals and avoid text-heavy slides.
